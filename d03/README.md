The text of the problem is available [here](https://adventofcode.com/2025/day/3)

#### What are we asked to find?

The total "joltage" of a bank of batteries

#### What do we know?

* The input is a series of lines, which each line corresponding to a bank of "batteries"
* Each battery is represented as a single digit
* Only two batteries can be activated per bank
* The total "joltage" provided by the bank is the number produced by the concatenation of the digits representing those batteries
* The order of the batteries cannot be changed
